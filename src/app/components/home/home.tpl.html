Design vs Semantic: how to keep them together without messing each-other

Common issues

  • Bind styles to tag selectors
    body section article h2 {...}
  • Use some tags because how they look instead of what they mean
    <h1>Main Article title</h1> instead of <h2 class="main__title">Main Article title</h2>
  • Apply the same styles to different selectors over and over
    nav li a {cursor: pointer;} and some lines after aside li a {cursor: pointer;}

Design vs Semantic: how to keep them together without messing each-other

A single and practical solution

Think Semantic (markup) and Design (CSS) apart. You'll find out your HTML will be more accurate in meaning and create components in CSS to use over and over regardless which element you target.
